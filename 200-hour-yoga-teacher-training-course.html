200 Hours Yoga Teacher Training Course in Rishikesh

The 200 hours yoga teacher training Course in Rishikesh Swami Vivekananda Yoga School, Rishikesh, India will be your optimal experience ever that we have differentiates into 4 section (28 days).The main objectives of this course are spreading yoga and meditation knowledge worldwide and providing the best yogic and meditation techniques to encourage the self-confidence and lifestyle.
These 28 days of systematic learning will enhance your practices and get you feel the complete Indian traditional yogic life. The course is committed to fulfilling your dreams that you see to become a professional yoga teacher who not only will teach the students but also will live a yogic life ahead.
This course is well designed with an appropriate yogic schedule that fits you and includes basic to advanced level yoga and meditation knowledge simultaneously. We believe that for the journey of becoming a yoga teacher, you have to be a yoga student yourself first where the definition of a yoga teacher is to be meant as 'the foundation of great yoga teacher's is becoming a great student first'.

200 Hours Yoga Teacher Training Course in Rishikesh - Week Wise Programs

  • The first week of this course is started from some basic practices and theories or the basic knowledge of yoga and its benefits.
  • In this week, we focus on basic asanas (yoga postures) to commence the habit of yoga practices. The seven classes of the first week will be great knowledge gaining for you to get ready and get involved in the whole month. The first-week increase your interest and let you know the mental and spiritual benefits of yoga and it solves the question "why should you join this course?"
  • The second week of yoga course will be focused on postures and gestures. This week you will be treated as intermediate students. Your pranayama (breathing techniques), ashtanga - vinyasa and meditation practices to controlling the body -mind.
  • The week is committed providing the knowledge of "how to control your body and mind"
  • The asanas you already have learned will be repaired in the week. You will also get flexibility and improve your postures alignments.
  • As you have completed the half journey of this course, third week will make you from good to best. The week is full of asanas corrections, alignment - adjustments. Apart from that, we also provide the knowledge of Indian Philosophy and Indian Spiritual Culture.
  • A jump from beginner to advanced will be done this week. This week is good for personality development as well as spiritual development in heart and mind. Yoga gestures will be taught deeply this week.
  • The fourth week of yoga course is focused on Teaching Methodology. This week is focused on the question "how to teach your beloved students"
  • Lifestyle, living style and yogic style, your all daily patterns should be changed because you are a yoga teacher now and you deserve to look like it. This week will completely change your thinking and your students will feel this when you start teaching.
  • Energy locks, basic Ayurveda, basic yoga therapy, yoga Nidra - Antar-Mauna, meditation (silent, meditate on OM), mantra chanting will be finished in the last week.
