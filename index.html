Welcome to
Swami Vivekanand Yoga Teacher Training Course & Meditation School in Rishikesh

Swami Vivekananda Yoga & Meditation School is situated in Rishikesh, which is known as “world Capital of Yoga.”The beauty, serenity and climate of this place features the ideally suitable environment for the practice of Yoga. Our Teachings are based on the five points of yoga—postures, breathing, relaxation, meditation, and philosophy. We offer drop-in classes, intensive-courses and yoga-teacher-training-course as well as courses in yoga and meditation.

Our yoga teacher training program gives our students an opportunity to live together, learning and growing as a family. The course is nurtured with love and compassion of our Teachers who guide the students physically, mentally and emotionally helping them break the barrier within as they encounter and understand Yoga as a whole through its various dimensions like the physical Asana practice, Breathing techniques, Yoga philosophy, Guided and active Meditation, Adjustment and alignment classes, Yogic cleansing techniques, Mudras and Bandhas, Yoga anatomy and many more aspects that makes our Yoga teacher training in India a complete syllabus for a beginner or an advanced practitioner [...]

Motivational Yoga Family

Every yoga instructor and staff member of S V Yoga School is well behaved and providing the best for each and every student. It is a family where you built love, strength and belief by the powers of yoga. You feel motivated after each yoga class.

We believe “Yoga is a light, which once lit, will never dim. The better your practice the brighter the flame”

Perfect Yoga Place

Our school is very close to near Ganga River, because of very close to Ganga River we take blessings from the Goddess Ganga with its magical and positive energy along with fresh air which help to transform your thoughts.

“Ganga is more than a river, she is Holy Mother ‘Ganga Maa’ ”

Perfect Alignment

As we know in yoga, a perfect posture is needed otherwise a wrong posture will effect your body. Yoga with proper alignment offer a way for students to correct their own poses. We focus this problem and managed two trained and qualified yoga teacher in one class.

We believe “Perfect Alignment often feels like the holy grail of any Vigorous yoga practice”.

Offer for our students

Those students did yoga from our school they are also part of our yoga family, after doing the 28 or 35 days yoga teacher training course. So, we organized a special opportunity only for our Ex-YTTC students.
If they want to do yoga classes at our place after finished the yoga teacher training or in future, they can do 5 yoga classes free of cost.
