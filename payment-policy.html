PAYMENT POLICY FOR FEE SUBMISSION

Your course will not be booked without accruing advance fees. The course will be booked only when, you deposit the advance fee prescribed for each course.

The Confirmation Fee of USD 400 (non-refundable) must be paid in advance for Yoga TTC - 300 hours,USD 210 (non-refundable) must be paid in advance for Yoga TTC - 200 hours, USD 100 for 15 days yoga intensive course, USD 50 for Reiki course and USD 25 for 3 - 5 -7 days intensive yoga course.

The PayPal Charges and Bank charges will be paid on arrival when we show our Bank/Paypal account statements.

Refund Policy

This is non-refundable but may be transferred to any other upcoming Yoga Teacher Training course with in one year. If you cannot come to our YTTC after paying the confirmation fee you can use it towards any future course or program.

The ConfirmationFee of Fee is the part of the entire course fee. The rest of the course fee balance must be paid in cash on the first day of the course either in Euros, US Dollars, British Pounds or INR (amount according to the Exchange rate on the date of payment).

NO REFUNDS, CREDITS OR TRANSFERS ARE AVAILABLE ON CANCELLATION AFTER THE START DATE OF ANY YOGA TEACHER TRAINING COURSE.
After start any course, Course fee will not be refundable.

About Us

Swami Vivekananda School is situated in Rishikesh, which is known as “world Capital of Yoga” The beauty, serenity and climate of this place features the ideally suitable environment for the practice of Yoga.

Our Teachings are based on the five points of yoga—postures, breathing, relaxation, meditation, and philosophy.
