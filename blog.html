EASY WAY OF LIFE :- PRACTICE

There are three parts of practice: morning meditation, evening cleaning and prayer-meditation at night.
Meditation means - constantly thinking about one thing and it is believed that whatever we want to achieve, we will make you the subject of meditation That is why what we have to do is sit down and close the eyes and think that our God is full of light.
Now we keep discussing the next thing, which is the process of cleaning in the evening. It has to be done after completing all the work of the day-not before. The way to clean yourself is that you sit for half an hour in comfort and suggesting yourself - imagine that with all the confusion and solidarity All the unwanted things, darkness, etc. are going out of your entire mechanism as a vapor on the back, and in place of all these divine sacred edges gurudev's heart is entering your heart. Do not pay attention to the things you want to get rid of. Just clear them.
The third thing is meditation at night. This is a simple thing. It is said to us that we should sit for about 10 minutes on our bed, once in a while, pray again in prayer and meditate on the meaning of prayer. This prayer is to be done by 10-15 or 20 minutes and then we fall asleep. At the end of the day we do this very last.
For the use of prayer, understand that before starting the meditation in the morning, it is only to say once. There is no place in the evening cleaning process.

THE SECRET POWER

As we follow Swami Vivekananda, here I am discuss one story of his that where is the secret power?
Once, in Heaven there was a discussion among the Gods to decide where the miraculous secret power, the power by which man can achieve anything in this world was to be kept hidden. One of the Gods suggested that it could be kept hidden in the depths of the Sea. Another said it could be buried on top of high mountains. The third one thought of a cave in the woods as the right place. At last, the most intelligent among them said, 'Keep it in the depths of man's mind. He will never suspect that the power is hidden there because right from his childhood, his mind is prone to wandering and he will not look within. Only the intelligent among them will look within, and use the power and become great.' All gods agreed. So, do you see that the difference between two people lies in their minds? One knows how to tap its power and the other does not. So, the truth expressed here is that in the depths of man's mind lies hidden the miraculous power, using which one can become great. Whatever you think, that you will be. If you think yourselves weak, weak you will be; if you think yourselves strong, strong you will be.
By Swami Vivekananda

The word ‘yoga’ comes from a Sanskrit root ‘yuj’ which means to join. In its spiritual sense it is that process by which the identity ofjivatma and parmatma is realized by the yogis. The human soul is brought into conscious communion with god. “ yoga is restraining the mental modifications. Yoga is that inhibition of the functions of the mind which leads to abidance of the spirit in his real nature.
Yoga is the science that teaches the method of joining the human spirit with god. Yoga is the divine science which disentangles the Jiva with the phenomenal world of sense objects and links him with the AnantaAnanda(Infinite Bliss), Parama Shanti(Supreme Peace), joy of an Akhanda character and power that are inherent attributes of the absolute. WHY START THE DAY WITH SURYA NAMASKARA?

Surya Namaskar is a set of 12 postures, preferably to be done at the time of sunrise. The regular practice of Surya Namaskar improves circulation of blood throughout the body, maintains health, and helps one remain disease-free. There are numerous benefits of Surya Namaskar for the heart, liver, intestine, stomach, chest, throat, legs. From head to toe, every part of the body is greatly benefitted by Surya Namaskar, which is why it is highly recommended by all yoga experts.
Postures act as a good link between warm-ups and asanas and can be done any time on an empty stomach. However, morning is considered to be the best time for Surya Namaskar as it revitalizes the body and refreshes the mind, making us ready to take on all tasks of the day. If done in the afternoon, it energizes the body instantly and if done at evening, it helps you unwind.
